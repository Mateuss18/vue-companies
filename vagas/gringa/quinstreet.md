# Vue.js Developer

Corporate Overview

Powering Performance Marketplaces in Digital Media

QuinStreet is a pioneer in powering decentralized online marketplaces that match searchers and research-and-compare consumers with brands. It operates virtual and private-label marketplaces in one of the nation’s largest media networks.

Its segmentation and AI-driven matching technologies help consumers find better solutions and help brands reach in-market prospects with precision and pay only for performance results. Those matching engines and optimization algorithms are built from more than 20 years and billions of dollars of online media experience.

Job Category

The company is seeking a Vue.js Developer with a passion for building exceptional user interfaces and delivering seamless user experiences. In this role, you will design and implement efficient, reusable, and scalable front-end components while adhering to best practices. You’ll collaborate closely with backend engineers to integrate APIs and ensure smooth functionality across the application.

Responsibilities

Contribute production code focused on security, reliability, availability, and scale.

Determine the best implementation to meet the solution architecture.

Work with Vue.js to design, develop, and debug web applications.

Perform requirements analysis, high-level design, and effort estimation for new requirements and enhancements.

Participate in design discussions with business users, architecture, and engineering teams.

Work closely with QA engineers, technical leads, and business users.

Solve technical problems and handle performance and scalability issues day to day.

Integrate backend services with frontend needs.

Help manage, optimize, and customize multiple web applications.

Create and maintain technical documentation.

Requirements

2+ years of software development experience, including object-oriented design and Vue.js development.

1+ year of hands-on experience with web application architecture and relational database design.

1+ year of experience building database-driven applications and websites.

Proficiency with HTML, DHTML, JavaScript, CSS, and AJAX.

Understanding of cross-platform and cross-browser compatibility concerns.

Experience with Vue.js development frameworks.

Strong presentation, communication, and collaboration skills.

Ability to handle a high task load, tight deadlines, and strong prioritization.

Knowledge of REST APIs.

The expected salary range is USD 90,000 to USD 125,000 annually, with eligibility for performance bonus or commission, restricted stock units, and standard employee benefits programs.
