# Junior Software Developer (Vue)

Job Type: Full-time.

K2United is an organization that houses two distinct, national, customer-facing brands tied together by a shared purpose: setting the standard for an extraordinary workplace. Through its brands, K2Share and CareerSafe, the group provides advisory services in cyber risk management and online education for workforce readiness.

About You

You are an early-career software developer with a strong interest in building user interfaces and delivering great user experiences. You enjoy turning ideas and designs into functional, intuitive applications and are motivated to grow your front-end development skills.

You are comfortable working within defined guidelines and established standards while learning how to improve and expand your capabilities. You care about writing clean, maintainable code and are developing an eye for usability, accessibility, and performance. You enjoy working closely with others, including designers, product managers, and engineers. You are open to feedback, eager to learn, and excited to contribute to a collaborative team environment.

Your Impact

As a Junior Software Developer, you will help build and maintain the user interface of our applications. You will work on well-defined tasks while gaining experience across development, testing, and collaboration.

Your impact will include writing, testing and maintaining UI code that meets engineering and design standards, delivering simple and high-quality solutions for assigned work items, implementing accessible, responsive and intuitive user interfaces with guidance, supporting usability and compatibility standards across applications, collaborating with product, design and QA teams, participating in Agile ceremonies, code reviews and pull request processes, and building a working understanding of the systems and UI architecture in your area.

Technical Skills

Foundational experience in front-end development with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Familiarity with modern frameworks such as Vue, React, Angular or similar.

Basic understanding of responsive design, accessibility fundamentals and browser compatibility.

Understanding of Git, testing concepts, API integration and backend services.

Exposure to tools such as GitHub, VS Code, Figma, Postman and Jira.

Benefits include 401(k), medical benefits, paid time off, wellness stipend, tuition stipend, casual dress environment, technical training and free CareerSafe online training courses for employees and immediate family.

Salary range: $60,000 - $67,000.
