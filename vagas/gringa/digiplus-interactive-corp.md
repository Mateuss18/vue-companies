# Senior Frontend Developer (Vue.js)

About DigiPlus Interactive Corp.

DigiPlus Interactive Corp. pioneered digital entertainment in the Philippines. It introduced leading platforms BingoPlus and ArenaPlus, widely known for their engaging experiences in interactive gaming and sports entertainment. DigiPlus also operates GameZone, with more to come.

Job Overview:

We are looking for a skilled Vue.js developer to join the team. The role focuses on developing high-quality applications and intuitive user interfaces with strong use of the Vue.js ecosystem.

Key Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain scalable web applications using Vue.js.
Build responsive user interfaces and ensure a seamless user experience.
Collaborate with back-end developers and other team members to integrate APIs and services.
Optimize applications for speed, scalability, and performance.
Create reusable components and libraries for future use.
Debug and resolve complex issues.
Participate in code reviews and follow industry best practices.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

Proven experience with Vue.js and its core principles, including Vue Router and the Composition API.
5+ years of experience.
Strong understanding of JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3.
Experience with front-end tooling like Webpack, Babel, and ESLint.
Familiarity with RESTful APIs, asynchronous programming, Git, and responsive applications.

Nice-to-Have:

TypeScript, SSR knowledge, and familiarity with Agile/Scrum.
