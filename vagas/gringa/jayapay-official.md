# Senior Frontend Developer- Payment Gateway (Vue js.）

Company Description

Jayapay is a global payment solutions provider that helps businesses expand across borders. Its platform supports pay-in and pay-out operations in multiple countries and industries through secure, compliant, and scalable infrastructure.

Responsibilities

Develop and maintain frontend projects for payment service systems, working closely with backend, QA, and product teams to ensure high-quality deliveries. Design reusable frontend components and shared libraries. Participate in frontend technology selection and architecture discussions. Translate business requirements into intuitive and efficient user experiences. Continuously optimize system performance, stability, and usability through refactoring and technical improvements.

Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree or above. More than 5 years of frontend development experience. Strong command of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6+, and TypeScript. Proficiency in at least one mainstream frontend framework such as Vue or React. Ability to independently design frontend solutions, build UI, create mock data, and integrate or debug APIs. Experience with performance optimization, rendering optimization, and cross-browser compatibility. Strong attention to UI and UX details. Excellent communication skills, teamwork, and proactive mindset.

Additional Requirement

No visa sponsorship required.
