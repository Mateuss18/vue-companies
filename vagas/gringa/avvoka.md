# Vue.js Frontend Senior Software Developer

We’re Avvoka

We build drafting technology that's transforming the way the world contracts - our platform helps legal teams move faster through contracts using automation and AI, without taking judgement away from the lawyers in control. We believe technology should amplify expertise, not replace it.

Avvoka is trusted by over 20% of the AmLaw 100 law firms, global banks and enterprises, and we've grown largely through product strength. With headcount and revenue continuing to scale rapidly year on year, we're now moving from a product-led path into a globally recognised legal-tech brand.

We're at an inflection point: evolving how the world's most sophisticated legal teams work — and building a company where thoughtful people can do the best work of their careers.

Why join us

This is a hands-on senior role in a product where the frontend really matters: lawyers spend hours a day in complex drafting and review workflows, so performance, clarity, and reliability are core to trust.

You’ll work closely with a pragmatic product and engineering team to ship improvements that users feel immediately, while also strengthening the foundations in Vue patterns, TypeScript consistency, testing, and UI scalability so the team can move faster as the product grows.

Role details

Department: Frontend

Engagement Focus: Individual Contributor

Primary Point of Contact: Frontend Lead

Location: Prague 6

Billable Hours: up to 160h a month

Compensation: Competitive, based on experience

Start Date: Flexible

What you’ll do

Build high-quality Vue.js features across complex workflow-heavy areas such as forms, editors, approvals, and automation UI.

Turn product requirements into pragmatic technical designs that balance speed, maintainability, and user experience.

Own work from implementation through release, including edge cases, tracking, and iterative improvements.

Improve performance, accessibility, and UI reliability in areas with high daily usage.

Strengthen component patterns and TypeScript practices so the codebase stays easy to change as the company scales.

Reduce regressions through sensible testing and code review discipline.

Collaborate closely with product, design, backend, and other teammates to simplify complexity and ship clear experiences.

Support the team with thoughtful reviews, shared patterns, and targeted tech debt reduction.

What success looks like

Meaningful frontend improvements with measurable user impact, stronger performance and reliability, and fewer regressions while maintaining delivery speed.

What you’ll bring

Strong production experience with Vue.js, ideally Vue 3.

Solid TypeScript experience and comfort designing maintainable component and state patterns.

Track record of shipping in a B2B product environment where correctness and usability matter.

Strong quality mindset across performance, accessibility, and maintainability.

Clear communication and contractor-style ownership.

Bonus points for experience with document-like interfaces, complex forms, permissioned workflows, Vue migrations, performance improvements, testing, or observability tooling.

Our hiring process includes CV review, screening call, assessment interview, a senior interview for senior roles, and where possible an in-person team meeting in Prague.
