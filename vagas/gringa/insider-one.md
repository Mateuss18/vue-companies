# Senior Frontend Developer - Vue.js

Before jumping into all the information about the role and what you can bring to the table, let us introduce ourselves real quick.

About Us

Insider One is the #1 platform that brings everything marketing and customer engagement teams need in one place so they can reach their peak potential and become unstoppable.

Our story began with six desks and a vision to create a single platform to make industry-first technologies and emerging channels accessible to marketers worldwide. Today, Insider One is powered by 1,500+ team members representing 50+ nationalities across 30+ offices. With AI at its core and an integrated Customer Data Platform (CDP), Insider One unites data, personalization, and journey orchestration across a broad set of natively supported channels, including WhatsApp, SMS, Email, Web, App, and Site Search.

We recently raised a $500M Series E led by General Atlantic and are trusted by 2,000+ customers, from high-growth startups to Fortune 500 companies such as Samsung, Nike, L’Oreal, Singapore Airlines, Nestlé, Nissan, Lenovo, Puma, IKEA, Allianz, and Domino's.

We are looking for a Front-end Developer who will help the Web Development Team create and implement a wide variety of web-based products using Go, Node JS, or PHP. You will join a dynamic team focused on innovative software solutions built with Native JavaScript, jQuery, Vue.js, TypeScript, and Node.js.

What You Will Do

Participate in the full project lifecycle from product development to maintenance. Develop and maintain high-quality, scalable JavaScript and TypeScript applications. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, work closely with back-end developers, maintain code quality through daily reviews, and write unit tests to ensure stability.

What You Will Need

5+ years of experience as a front-end developer with modern DevOps tooling, strong proficiency in Vue.js, TypeScript, and Node.js, experience with cloud services, testing frameworks, design patterns, RESTful APIs, responsive design, and version control systems such as Git.

What We Offer

Monthly meal allowance, private health insurance, access to learning platforms, internal trainings, a global team, ESOP eligibility, referral bonuses, volunteering opportunities, team events, and a fully remote setup within Turkey.
