# Fullstack Engineer (PHP/Vue.js)

Who we're looking for: An experienced Software Engineer with a proven ability to work with Vue.js and PHP.

Where you'll work: This role will be based in Cape Town, you'll be part of a global team, collaborating with colleagues and serving customers across the UK, USA, Australia, South Africa, and beyond. The hybrid approach offers flexibility while keeping team connection in the Cape Town office three days a week.

The role includes designing and building new features within the UX/UI team, integrating third-party services and APIs, performing code reviews, collaborating with Product and Design to scope and estimate work, maintaining high code quality, and supporting other engineers through regular pairing sessions.

The description highlights experience with Nuxt/Vue.js, PHP/Laravel, Tailwind CSS, Storybook, Git, strong communication, and comfort in fast-paced environments. It also lists AWS, DataDog, SQL design, TDD, scalable systems, Agile/Scrum, and responsible use of AI tools as desirable extras.

About Tillo

Tillo makes gift cards, rewards, and incentives simple, efficient, and profitable. Operating in more than 37 markets and 25 currencies, the company processes billions in gift card transactions through a plug-and-go API for leading global businesses.
