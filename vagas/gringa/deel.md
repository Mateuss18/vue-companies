# Senior Full Stack Engineer ( .NET/ Vue)

Deel is the all-in-one payroll and HR platform for global teams. The company positions itself as infrastructure for the future of work, combining HRIS, payroll, compliance, benefits, performance, and equipment management in a single platform supported by AI and owned payroll infrastructure.

The role sits inside a cross-functional team of frontend, backend, full-stack engineers, product, design, and QA. The description highlights participation across the whole software development lifecycle, from product discovery and planning through deployment, with emphasis on collaboration, API work, bug fixing, and customer experience.

The position requires deep knowledge of C# and .NET, .NET Core, Entity Framework, MS-SQL, Azure DevOps, RESTful APIs, and at least five years of software engineering experience. Deel also expects candidates to have prior frontend exposure and willingness to work with Vue.js at production level, plus strong communication, remote collaboration, and customer-focused engineering judgment.

The job mentions extra value for experience with Vue.js, JavaScript or TypeScript, Redis, microservices, NoSQL, and fintech. It also reinforces the company’s remote culture, stock-related compensation options, and globally distributed operating model.
