# Senior Laravel/Vue.js Developer

We're Comeen, our mission is to create a seamless experience between people and workplaces.

We help 300+ companies worldwide implement modern workplace environments with communication and workplace management tools. Our SaaS platform integrates with Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, Slack and offers:

Corporate digital signage
In-office days planning
Desk booking
Meeting room booking
Visitor check-in

Comeen was founded in 2018 and has grown significantly in recent years. The team has 30+ people and works from Bordeaux, Paris and Austin.

Tasks

As a senior fullstack developer, you will join the development team and work on Comeen Play, the digital signage platform, building third-party integrations, administration dashboards and the broadcasting web app.

The role covers backend engineering in Laravel, frontend engineering in Vue.js, ecosystem integrations with providers such as Google Workspace and Microsoft 365, architectural decisions for performance and scalability, code quality through testing and reviews, and technical leadership through mentorship and process improvement.

Requirements

5+ years of professional experience with Laravel and expert knowledge of the framework.
5+ years of professional experience with Vue.js and expert knowledge of the ecosystem.
Strong proficiency in MySQL and Redis.
Experience with RESTful APIs, third-party integrations, CI/CD and Git.
Experience with Google Cloud Platform, Electron, Android or Chrome extensions is a plus.

Benefits

Restaurant vouchers
Two in-person events per year
Mobility support
Health insurance
Three days per week remote working policy
