# Mid Full Stack Developer (Laravel & Vue)

Company Description

Ambition Guru is an AI-powered learning platform focused on helping students and professionals unlock their potential and improve academic performance. The product offers courses, mock tests, study materials, and performance analysis across academic, professional, and language-preparation tracks.

Key Responsibilities

Develop, maintain, and improve scalable web applications using Laravel. Build responsive, reusable, and modular Vue.js components for frontend applications. Integrate RESTful APIs and third-party services. Write clean, documented, and testable code. Participate in code reviews, team meetings, and product planning. Optimize the application for speed and scalability. Troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing software. Collaborate with DevOps on CI/CD, testing, and deployment.

Required Skills

2 to 3 years of hands-on experience with Laravel. Strong experience with Vue.js, Vuex, Vue Router, and component-based architecture. Proficiency with MySQL and MongoDB. Experience with RESTful APIs and JSON. Familiarity with Git, GitHub or GitLab workflows. Knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern build tools such as Webpack or Vite. Basic understanding of authentication, authorization, and security best practices. Working knowledge of Docker, Nginx, Redis, or queue workers. Familiarity with Agile and Scrum. Experience with unit and integration testing.

Education

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or equivalent experience.
