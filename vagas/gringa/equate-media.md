# Senior Full Stack Developer - (Vue / Node.js)

This is a 100% Remote Independent Contractor Role.

About Equate Media

Equate Media is a growth-focused digital company that builds software and operational systems designed to improve efficiency, support customer engagement, and drive measurable business results. Our teams work closely across engineering, marketing, and operations to solve practical problems, improve workflows, and create systems that make a real impact. We value speed, ownership, adaptability, and people who enjoy building useful things in a fast-moving environment.

About the role

We’re hiring a Senior Full Stack Developer to help strengthen and expand a real-time communications platform used every day by the business. This person will step into an existing production environment, work through active issues, improve platform reliability, and help push new functionality into production quickly.

The work touches both frontend and backend systems tied to inbound and outbound call activity, routing logic, internal buffering, operational tooling, and live user workflows. This is a strong fit for someone who enjoys solving real production problems, working across the stack, and making meaningful contributions without a lot of hand-holding.

Where you’ll make an impact

Get up to speed quickly in an existing multi-service production platform
Investigate and resolve issues across frontend, backend, and third-party integrations
Deliver new features at a high pace while keeping stability and maintainability in mind
Improve systems that support real-time call flows, routing behavior, and internal buffering
Build and refine interfaces used for live operations and communication workflows
Develop backend services, APIs, and platform functionality tied directly to business performance
Help improve visibility, reliability, and overall behavior in a live production environment
Contribute to engineering decisions and help raise execution standards across the team

Tools and environment

Frontend: Vue 3, TypeScript, Vite, Pinia, Vue Router
Backend: Node.js, JavaScript, TypeScript, Express
Real-time / Communications: Socket.IO, WebRTC, browser based calling with Twilio/Sinch/similar system
Infrastructure / Services: AWS, Redis, RabbitMQ / AMQP, PM2
Monitoring / Error Tracking: Sentry

What we’re looking for

5+ years of experience as a full stack software developer
Strong backend experience with Node.js
Strong frontend experience with Vue, or strong experience with a modern frontend framework and the ability to work confidently in a Vue codebase
Experience working in existing production systems, not only creating from scratch
Ability to troubleshoot across the stack and solve real-world issues efficiently
Experience shipping features quickly in fast-moving environments
Strong instincts around performance, reliability, and maintainability
Comfortable taking ownership and operating independently
Experience with AWS or other cloud-based production environments
Strong communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively across teams

Bonus Points For

Telephony, VoIP, call routing, or communications platforms
Twilio, Sinch, or similar providers
Real-time systems, event-driven architecture, queues, or worker-based services
Redis, RabbitMQ, or live operational workflows
Improving legacy systems while continuing to ship new functionality
Exposure to AI-assisted workflows such as transcription, summarization, or automation

Why this role stands out

This is an opportunity to work on a live platform that supports real operational activity and directly impacts the business. You’ll be solving practical problems, improving systems that people rely on every day, and helping shape what gets built next. If you enjoy working in production systems, fixing meaningful problems, and shipping useful features quickly, we’d love to hear from you.
