# Senior Fullstack Engineer (Python/Vue)

About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Senior Fullstack Engineer with strong skills in Python and Vue to join our team. In this role, you will work across the entire stack to build, integrate, and optimize high-quality web applications. You will also play a key part in mentoring junior engineers and ensuring smooth collaboration between frontend and backend development.

Key Responsibilities

Develop and integrate frontend and backend features using Python and Vue.

Rapidly prototype new ideas and solutions to support product development.

Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to UI, APIs, and system integrations.

Support and mentor junior engineers, especially in debugging full-stack interactions.

Basic Qualifications

Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.

5+ years of professional experience in full-stack development.

Proven experience as a Fullstack Engineer, ideally with Python (Django, FastAPI or Flask) and Vue.

Ability to quickly prototype and iterate on ideas.

Strong problem-solving skills across UI, API, and integration layers.

Familiarity with debugging and optimizing frontend-backend communication.

Excellent communication skills and ability to guide junior team members.
