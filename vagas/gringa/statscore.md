# Mid Fullstack JavaScript Developer (Node & Vue)

STATSCORE is one of the world's leading companies in innovative products for the sports industry. The company collects, organizes and delivers high-quality sports data that helps sports enthusiasts, media and betting organizations achieve their goals.

All of its products are built in-house and are strictly related to sports, including live visualizations, sports statistics widgets and raw data provided by SportsAPI.

Role

As a Mid Developer, you will join the R&D group to solve complex problems in a simple way. The company is looking for long-term cooperation and values quality engineering, teamwork and empathy.

You will cooperate with other Developers, Product Manager, QA, System Architect and Tech Leader.

Technologies

NestJS, Redis, MySQL, RabbitMQ, Vue.js, Sass, Tailwind CSS, PWA, WebSocket, Docker, Git, CI/CD and GitHub Actions.

Responsibilities

Take an active part in creating the technology roadmap.

Participate in the development cycle, including coding, code review and unit tests.

Build products ensuring high performance, scalability and reliability.

Promote good practices, code quality and technical debt control.

Requirements

4+ years of experience building modern JavaScript applications in a commercial environment.

Knowledge of HTML, CSS/SCSS, ES6+, TypeScript, Node.js and Vue.js or another framework.

Experience with architecture and software design patterns.

Good interpersonal skills and fluent English at B1 level or above.

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Understanding of Agile methodologies, mainly Scrum and Kanban.

The company works in hybrid mode with flexible hours, from home or from the office in Katowice.

Salary range: 9,500 - 16,500 PLN gross CoE.
