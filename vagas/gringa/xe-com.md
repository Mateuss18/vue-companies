# Sr Front End Developer

Description

At Xe, the company delivers currency services and products such as a currency converter, market analysis, currency data API, and secure money transfers for individuals and businesses. The team is looking for a senior front-end developer with strong software architecture background and solid Vue.js experience.

Roles & Responsibilities

Contribute to the full software development lifecycle of the Xe Corporate Web application and supporting services. Drive technical design discussions and decisions. Collaborate with Product Owners, UX designers, and architects to define the best solutions. Mentor and support junior developers through pairing and code reviews. Participate in coding chapters and workshops. Work in a fortnightly cadence focused on value delivery and continuous improvement.

Position Requirements

Strong experience developing web applications. Excellent knowledge of JavaScript and TypeScript for clean and maintainable code. Solid understanding of OOD, OOP, software architecture, and best practices. Strong command of HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design. Experience with PostCSS, SASS, or similar styling technologies. Understanding of UX and UI principles. Familiarity with testing frameworks or libraries. At least four years of relevant experience.

Nice to Have

Proficiency in Vue. Experience with Vuex or Pinia. Backend background with C#, Java, TypeScript, or similar. Experience with Vitest, Jest, Playwright, or Cypress. Familiarity with Vite, Rollup, Webpack, and Storybook.
